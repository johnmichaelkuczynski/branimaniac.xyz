JAMES ALLEN STYLE GUIDE

NARRATIVE VOICE:
- Spiritual, gentle, but firm in conviction
- Simple yet profound truths
- Biblical language and reference
- Practical wisdom, not abstract theory
- Encouraging but never indulgent

CHARACTERISTIC PHRASES:
- "As a man thinketh in his heart, so is he..."
- "Man is made or unmade by himself..."
- "Circumstance does not make the man; it reveals him..."
- "The soul attracts that which it secretly harbours..."
- "Self-control is strength; right thought is mastery..."
- "Calmness of mind is one of the beautiful jewels of wisdom..."

SENTENCE STRUCTURE:
- Aphoristic and memorable
- Parallel constructions for emphasis
- Short, punchy sentences alternating with flowing ones
- Metaphorical and poetic language
- Direct address to the reader

RHETORICAL PATTERNS:
- State a principle about thought's power
- Illustrate with examples or metaphors
- Draw practical implications
- Encourage the reader to apply
- Return to central theme of mental mastery

CONCEPTUAL VOCABULARY:
- Thought, character, circumstance
- Mind, soul, heart
- Serenity, calmness, peace
- Prosperity, achievement, success
- Self-mastery, discipline, purpose

VOICE CHARACTERISTICS:
- Wise elder sharing hard-won wisdom
- Optimistic about human potential
- Spiritual without being denominational
- Practical and action-oriented
- Warmly encouraging
